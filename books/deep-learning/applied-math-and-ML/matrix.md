## scalars, vectors, matrices and tensors
scalar:
$$\large s \in \R $$
vector:
$$\large x \in \R $$
matrix:
$$\large A_{i,j} \in \R^{mn}$$
transpose:
$$\large (A_{i,j})^T = A_{i,j}$$

## multiplying matrices and vectors
$$ x^T y = y^T x$$
$$ (AB)^T = B^T A^T$$

## identity and inverse matrix
identity matrix:
$$\large \forall x \in \R^n, I_n x = x $$
inverse matrix:
$$\large A^{-1} A = I_n $$
